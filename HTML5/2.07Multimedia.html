Video element <video>

  • Video tags uses a src attribute or embedded <source>
  • Common attributes
    • height,width:
      how big your video would you like to appear on your browser?
    • autoplay:
      dictates whether or not the video should start as soon as you load the page.
    • loop:
      allow the video to play over and over.
    • cntrols:
      is something you can give the person more control over when to stop and start over the video.
  • Text inside <video>...</video> is displayed if browser cannot support tag

If you use a video tag it's good to put some text in there along with the different attributes. That way if the browser for some reason does not support the tag or can't play the video,you get a kind of helpful hint to the user letting them know there should be a video here that you would be looking at.

Audio element <audio>

  • Audio tag uses a src attribute to link to audio file,typically .mp3 or .wav
  • Common attributes
    • autoplay,controls,loop
    • buffered
    • muted
    • volume

Setting clips

  • You can set both the video and audio elements to play clips by adding to the src attribute.
    • .ext#t=5,25
      start at 5 sec and stop at 25 sec
    • .ext#t=,39
      start at begining and stop at 39 sec.
    • .ext#t=,1:38:45
      I want to start at one minute and 38 seconds,and just go ahead and play to 45.
    • .ext#t=42
      start at 45 second and play from there.
